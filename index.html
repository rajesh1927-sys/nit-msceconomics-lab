About MSc Economics Lab

The MSc Economics Lab at NIT Kurukshetra serves as a central platform for academic resources, data analysis, econometric training, and research activities. It integrates teaching, computation, and applied economic analysis using modern tools like R and Stata.

Programme Overview

The M.Sc. Economics programme provides strong foundations in economic theory, quantitative methods, econometrics, and applied research. The programme consists of coursework, lab training, electives, and dissertation work.
